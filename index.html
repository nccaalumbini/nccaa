Our Legacy

Rooted in the NCC Ethos Since 2022 B.S.

Introduction

The National Cadet Corps Alumni Association (NCCAA) is a non-partisan, service-driven body of former NCC cadets of Nepal, united by discipline, integrity, and duty to nation.

Rooted in the legacy of the NCC established in 2022 B.S., and operating in alignment with the Government of Nepal, the Nepal Army's NCC Directorate, and applicable laws, policies, and directives, the Association mobilizes trained alumni for national priorities.

We organize, equip, and deploy a reliable volunteer network for disaster risk reduction, community service, youth leadership development, and civic education.

Perpetuating the NCC Ethos

  • Discipline
  • Honesty and Loyalty
  • Leadership
  • Nationalism
  • National Unity

Our Mission

Guiding principles that define our purpose

  • Mobilize NCC-trained alumni as a disciplined national volunteer force for disaster preparedness, relief, and recovery.

  • Advance character and leadership through structured training, mentoring, seminars, and youth programs that reflect NCC standards.

  • Promote national unity and civic responsibility, undertaking social initiatives that directly serve communities and the State's legitimate needs.

  • Coordinate lawfully and effectively with the Government of Nepal, the Nepal Army (NCC Directorate), and partner institutions for planning, execution, and oversight.

  • Sustain an enduring alumni network, fostering fellowship, professional opportunity, and continuous readiness to serve.
